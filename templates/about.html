About The Dataset

Description: The dataset contains information collected from an online food ordering platform over a period of time. It encompasses various attributes related to Occupation, Family Size, Feedback etc..

The Source of this data: Source Link

Variables Information:

Title: Title of games.
Release_Date: Release Date of Games.
Developers: Companies/Developers of the game.
Summary: Summary of the game.
Platforms: Platforms of the game.
Genres: The genre of the games.
Rating: Rating of the game from 0 to 5.
